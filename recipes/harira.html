Moroccan Harira

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lamb or beef, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh celery leaves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup dried lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 1/2 cup dried chickpeas, soaked overnight
  • 6 cups chicken or beef broth
  • 1/4 cup uncooked vermicelli noodles
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  • Heat some oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the lamb or beef and cook until browned on all sides, then remove and set aside.
  • Add the onion and garlic to the pot and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  • Add the chickpeas, diced tomatoes, parsley, cilantro, celery leaves, cumin, ginger, smoked paprika, cinnamon, turmeric, and black pepper to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  • Add the lentils, soaked chickpeas, and chicken or beef broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 1 hour, or until the lentils are tender.
  • Add the vermicelli noodles to the pot and cook for another 10 minutes.
  • Stir in the lemon juice and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • To serve, ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with additional parsley or cilantro, if desired.
