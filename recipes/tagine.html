Moroccan lamb Tagine

Description

This lamb tagine recipe is a delicious Moroccan stew made with tender pieces of lamb, potatoes, peas, and a variety of spices. It's the perfect comfort food for a chilly evening or a special occasion.

To make this lamb tagine, you'll need the following ingredients:

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of boneless lamb, cut into cubes
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups of lamb or chicken broth
  • 2 cups of diced potatoes
  • 1 cup of frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon of ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot or dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add the lamb and cook until browned on all sides, about 8-10 minutes.
  2. Remove the lamb from the pot and set aside. Add the onions and garlic to the pot and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the lamb back to the pot, along with the broth, potatoes, peas, cilantro, parsley, cumin, coriander, paprika, ginger, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring the mixture to a simmer and reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and let it simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the lamb is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.
  5. Remove the lid and simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes to thicken the sauce.
  6. Serve the lamb tagine over rice or couscous and garnish with additional cilantro or parsley, if desired.
